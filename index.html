Who Am I?

Hi there! I'm Ahmet, a curious learner engaging in how people learn, think, and develop over time. I study English Language English as a Second Language (ESL) at Gazi University, though I also ponder about educational design, critical thinking, and optimized learning.

In this blog, you will find me writing on various subjects ,not only about language learning but also art, often learning with you as I do so. I’ve participated in volunteer programs and contributed to academic initiatives to expand my horizons and because I believe in learning by doing.

This space is not only for polished writings, it's for experiments, ideas, and sharing things for everyone. Whether you’re an educator, learner, or simply curious: Welcome!
